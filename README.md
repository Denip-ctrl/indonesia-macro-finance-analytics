# Indonesia Macro-Finance Analytics

A data-driven analytics platform built with Python to analyze and synthesize the complex financial landscape of Indonesia. This project employs a top-down investment research approach, tracking national economic health down to individual corporate performance on the Indonesia Stock Exchange (IDX).

### Key Modules & Capabilities:
* **Macroeconomic Efficiency Engine:** Tracking structural economic indicators, including historical ICOR (Incremental Capital Output Ratio) analysis, monetary liquidity (M0/M2 supply), and national growth prospects.
* **Global Commodity Correlation Matrix:** Real-time and historical correlation mapping between global commodity prices (e.g., Coal, Nickel) and cyclical IDX equities.
* **Quantitative Equity Analytics:** Advanced fundamental evaluation tools for assessing individual public companies listed on the IDX.
* **Interactive Intelligence Hub:** Seamlessly integrated with Streamlit to deliver high-performance, full-screen data visualizations and actionable insights for global stakeholders.
